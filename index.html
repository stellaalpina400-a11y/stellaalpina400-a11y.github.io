FAQ

Is there a watermark on the Free plan?

Yes, the Free plan adds a bottom-strip watermark. Pro and Studio have no watermark.

Does Pro/Studio include Face enhancement?

Yes. Face enhancement is included in Pro and Studio at no extra cost. It’s best for portraits and selfies: toggle Face enhancement in the app, then upload your image to get cleaner skin, eyes and hair details.

How fast is Pixweave?

Most images process in ~20-40 seconds on our backend. Very large images may take a bit longer.

What upscales are available?

Choose 2× or 4× before you start. Free supports 2×; Pro and Studio support 4×.

How many images can I process on each plan?

Free: up to 3 images/day. Pro: up to 150 images/month. Studio: up to 1000 images/month.

What can I use Pixweave for?

Upscale photos, illustrations and scans for sharing, printing, or design work. Use 2×/4×; turn Face enhancement on for people shots.

How do I pay?

We use PayPal checkout. You can pay with your PayPal balance or a bank card via PayPal.
